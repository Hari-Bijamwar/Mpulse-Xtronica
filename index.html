About us

P.E.S.’s Modern College of Engineering was established in June 1999, approved by AICTE New Delhi. The Institute has been awarded the ‘Best Professional College in Urban area’ by SPPU and accredited by NAAC with ‘A++’ grade with CGPA 3.66 in the year 2022. With the vision to impart holistic Education in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering to create engineers equipped to meet the challenges of a dynamic, global environment, the Department of E & TC was started in 1999. A flagship technological event called ‘M-PULSE XTRONICA’ has been organised by the E&TC Department since 2006 and also this year. Throughout the event, students will have great opportunities to showcase their skills, talent and get useful experience. There will be numerous technical and non-technical events, competitions and workshops.
